Introduction

Automate appointment scheduling, confirmations, and reminders for your service-based business. Save time and reduce no-shows.

Step-by-Step Guide

  • Create a booking form using Google Forms, Calendly, or Typeform.
  • Connect the form to n8n via webhook.
  • Set up automated email or WhatsApp confirmations.
  • Schedule reminder messages before the appointment.
  • Optionally, track responses and cancellations automatically.
