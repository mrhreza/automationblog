Introduction

Automate order confirmation, invoice generation, and delivery notifications for your small online store—even if using WhatsApp or manual cash-on-delivery sales.

Step-by-Step Guide

  • Create an order form using Google Forms or a simple website form.
  • Connect the form to n8n to process incoming orders.
  • Generate invoice PDFs automatically using available nodes or scripts.
  • Send confirmation messages via email or WhatsApp.
  • Maintain order records in Google Sheets for easy tracking.
