Introduction

Automate your Gmail workflow to save time. Send receipts, auto-respond to inquiries, and organize your inbox using AI and n8n workflows.

Step-by-Step Guide

  • Connect Gmail to n8n via OAuth authentication.
  • Create triggers for incoming emails (e.g., certain subject lines).
  • Set automated responses for inquiries or receipts.
  • Label and categorize emails automatically.
  • Generate reports on incoming messages for insights.
