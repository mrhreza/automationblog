Introduction

Automatically collect customer feedback and analyze trends to improve your products or services. This workflow saves time and provides actionable insights.

Step-by-Step Guide

  • Create a feedback form using Google Forms or Typeform.
  • Connect the form submissions to n8n using webhook triggers.
  • Store responses in Google Sheets or Airtable automatically.
  • Set up notifications for new feedback and analyze trends.
  • Use AI or spreadsheet formulas to summarize insights.
