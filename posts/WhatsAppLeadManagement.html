Introduction

Capture leads from WhatsApp automatically into Google Sheets. Organize them, follow up systematically, and never miss a potential customer.

Step-by-Step Guide

  • Connect WhatsApp to n8n using a supported integration or API.
  • Set up triggers to capture incoming lead messages.
  • Store lead information automatically in Google Sheets.
  • Use conditional logic to categorize leads based on interest or region.
  • Optionally, send automated follow-up messages to leads.
