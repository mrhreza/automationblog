Introduction

Track your inventory and orders automatically. Integrate online orders with Google Sheets or Airtable to generate reports and alerts when stock is low.

Step-by-Step Guide

  • Connect your sales form or e-commerce platform to n8n.
  • Store incoming orders in Google Sheets or Airtable.
  • Set up automated low-stock alerts via email or WhatsApp.
  • Create daily or weekly summary reports automatically.
  • Analyze inventory trends to plan restocking.
